TYPE SHOOTER GAME

Welcome to TYPE SHOOTER!


In this typing game, you control a spaceship at the bottom of the screen. Enemy ships appear with words beside them. To destroy them, type the word before they reach the bottom of the screen. Every correct word fires a laser from your ship. If an enemy gets past you, you lose!

How to play

  1. Enemies appear with words floating beside them.
  2. Type the word exactly as shown.
  3. Your ship shoots automatically when the word is typed correctly.
  4. Stop enemies from reaching the bottom of the screen. If they do, it's game over!

Game features

  • Test your typing speed under pressure.
  • Simple, clean visuals so you can focus on the words.
  • Score points for every enemy you destroy.
  • Multi enemy types with different words and speeds.

Audio controls

  • Background music starts automatically when you begin the game
  • You can mute the music by pressing the Mute button in the top-right corner.
  • Toggle sound on/off anytime during gameplay
